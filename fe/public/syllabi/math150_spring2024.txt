MATH-150: Calculus I - Spring 2024
SDSU Mathematics Department

Instructor: Dr. James Wilson
Email: jwilson@sdsu.edu
Office Hours: TTh 10:00 AM-12:00 PM

Course Description:
Differential calculus including limits, derivatives, and applications. Introduction to integral calculus.

Learning Objectives:
- Understand limits and continuity
- Calculate derivatives using various techniques
- Apply derivatives to optimization problems
- Understand the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

Grading:
- Exams (3 x 20% = 60%)
- Final Exam (25%)
- Homework (15%)

Required Materials:
- Calculus: Early Transcendentals (9th Edition) by Stewart
- Scientific calculator (graphing calculator recommended)
- WebAssign access code

Schedule:
Week 1-2: Limits and Continuity
Week 3-4: Derivatives and Rules
Week 5: Exam 1
Week 6-7: Applications of Derivatives
Week 8-9: Related Rates and Optimization
Week 10: Exam 2
Week 11-12: Integration Basics
Week 13-14: Fundamental Theorem of Calculus
Week 15: Exam 3
Week 16: Final Exam
